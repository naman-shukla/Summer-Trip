🌍 Summer Expedition '26

Nice, Milan & The Dolomites

A detailed 9-day journey traversing the French Riviera, the fashion capital of Italy, and the breathtaking alpine peaks. July 17th to July 25th.

⚠

Crucial Note for UAE Residents (Dubai Flights)

Since you are flying out of Dubai (DXB), you must acquire an International Driving Permit (IDP) in the UAE prior to your trip if your driver's license is non-EU. Italian law strictly mandates an IDP alongside your national license for all car rentals.

Trip Architecture

This plan balances Mediterranean seaside relaxation with heavy-duty mountain sightseeing, using high-speed rail to effortlessly connect France and Lombardy before entering South Tyrol.

🌊

Phase 1: French Riviera

July 17 - July 20

Base: Nice. Monaco coastal routes, scenic medieval cliffside villages, and beautiful beach views.

🚅

Phase 2: Lombardy

July 20 - July 21

Cross-border train transit along the coast to Milan Centrale. Experience the fashion center.

🏔

Phase 3: The Dolomites

July 21 - July 25

Base: Val Gardena. Dynamic exploration of passes, viewpoints (Seceda & Tre Cime) and high meadows.
