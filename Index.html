Dubai Driver's Compliance Checklist

Driving on public highways in Italy is highly organized, but camera enforcement is exceptionally strict. Make sure you complete this checklist before boarding your flight from Dubai:

1. International Driving Permit (IDP)

Mandatory: In Italy, your standard physical UAE/Dubai driver's license is legally insufficient on its own. You must carry an official International Driving Permit. Apply online via the ATC UAE website or RTA app (approx. 150 AED). Italian car rental desks at Venice VCE will refuse key handovers without it.

2. Camera-Enforced ZTL Zones

Avoid Tickets: Historic centers in Como, Milan, and Bolzano are camera-controlled restricted zones ("Zona a Traffico Limitato" or ZTL). Entering these without a resident permit results in automated €110+ tickets. Keep your car parked in designated underground municipal lots outside historical city arches.

3. Highway Toll Ticket Handling

Autostrada Fares: When entering Italian toll-ways (indicated by green signs), press the button and pull the paper ticket from the machine slot. Keep this safe. When exiting, select lanes marked with blue signs (representing Card payments) and slide in your ticket followed by your credit card.
